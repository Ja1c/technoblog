About the Start-up

Our app aims to revolutionize pet adoption by offering a unique, personalized experience that benefits both rescue centers and adopters. Too often, animals are overlooked on social media because they get lost in the flood of posts, with recent pets getting all the attention. We wanted to change that by designing an app that works like a dating platform, matching adopters with pets based on their lifestyle, preferences, and the pet's personality.

For adopters, you can input your personality traits, lifestyle, and preferences for a pet's temperament, such as whether the animal is good with kids or other pets. The app will then provide a list of animals that match your needs, making it easier to find a companion who will fit perfectly into your life.

For rescue centers, they can create detailed profiles of each animal, including information like age, color, temperament, and more. This helps potential adopters find pets that align with their preferences. Additionally, rescue centers can verify adopters to ensure the safety and well-being of the animals, making the entire process smoother and more reliable.

Our goal is to make pet adoption not only easier but also more efficient by ensuring every pet has a chance to find the right home, no matter how they look or where they’re listed. Plus, our app will continue to provide adopters with support after they’ve brought their new pet home—whether it’s reminders for vet visits, tips on pet care, or a community space where new pet parents can share their experiences.
