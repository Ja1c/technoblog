Market Sizing Framework: Evaluating TAM, SAM, and SOM for ADOPTABLE

Analyzing market sizing concepts to assess the growth potential and strategic direction for ADOPTABLE.

Market sizing is an essential process for understanding the scope and potential of a business within its target market. For ADOPTABLE, this involves analyzing the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) to determine growth opportunities in the pet adoption sector. This article breaks down these concepts and discusses their practical applications to guide ADOPTABLE’s strategy.

Defining Market Sizing: TAM, SAM, and SOM

Understanding market sizing is crucial for gauging the potential reach of ADOPTABLE. Below are the definitions of the three critical market sizing metrics:

  • Total Addressable Market (TAM): This represents the total revenue opportunity available in the pet adoption industry if ADOPTABLE were to capture 100% of the market. It includes the number of pets in need of adoption and the entire pool of potential adopters across regions and countries.
  • Serviceable Available Market (SAM): This is the portion of TAM that is within ADOPTABLE's serviceable capabilities. It reflects the geographical regions and specific demographics where ADOPTABLE can effectively operate, such as urban areas in the Philippines where adoption resources are more concentrated.
  • Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM): This is the practical share of SAM that ADOPTABLE can capture in the near term, taking into account factors like brand strength, marketing efforts, competition, and available resources. It’s the portion of the market that ADOPTABLE aims to acquire in the short and medium term.

Strategic Importance of Market Sizing for ADOPTABLE

For ADOPTABLE, accurate market sizing provides a clear picture of the total market potential and helps identify the most viable segments to target. By analyzing TAM, SAM, and SOM, ADOPTABLE can assess its current position, allocate resources efficiently, and focus on strategies that maximize growth. This process also allows for tracking progress and adjusting tactics as the company scales.

Applying Market Sizing Insights to ADOPTABLE's Strategy

  • By estimating TAM, ADOPTABLE gains insight into the overall opportunity in the pet adoption space.
  • SAM helps ADOPTABLE focus on the regions and market segments where it can most effectively serve, ensuring resources are used optimally.
  • With a clear understanding of SOM, ADOPTABLE can set measurable growth targets and refine its marketing and operational strategies to achieve them.

Market sizing is not just about identifying potential; it’s about strategically positioning ADOPTABLE to succeed. By understanding and applying the concepts of TAM, SAM, and SOM, ADOPTABLE can build a clear path for growth, target the right customers, and optimize its efforts to become a leader in the pet adoption industry.
