Adoptable: Simplifying Pet Adoption

Key Partners

Adoptable partners with the LGU, CDO Animal Welfare Society, City Veterinary Office, and Provincial Veterinary Office to ensure support for the app’s operations. These key players provide resources for animal care and local engagement.

Key Activities

Core activities include app development, marketing, online promotion, customer service, and app maintenance. These ensure a smooth user experience and continuous growth.

Value Proposition

Adoptable simplifies the adoption process by offering personalized pet matches based on lifestyle and compatibility. This tailored approach creates more successful, long-term pet placements.

Customer Relationships

Users benefit from freebies, charitable donation opportunities, and online communities, enhancing engagement beyond the adoption process.

Channels

Adoptable reaches users through social media, app stores, pet organizations, and local government channels, ensuring visibility for both adopters and rescue centers.

Customer Segments

The app targets pet owners, first-time adopters, and animal rescue organizations. Each group benefits from the platform’s matching and management features.

Key Resources

The app relies on veterinarians, developers, database admins, and social workers to ensure the quality and functionality of its services.

Cost Structure

Costs include app development, deployment, labor, pet supplies, and maintenance, all crucial to keep the platform running smoothly.

Revenue Streams

Adoptable generates income through ads, sponsorships, commissions, and subscriptions, diversifying its revenue sources to support long-term growth.
