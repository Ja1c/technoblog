About Us

We are a dedicated team from BSIT-3R10 developing a pet adoption app aimed at streamlining the adoption process for both rescue centers and potential adopters. Our mission is to tackle common challenges in the pet adoption landscape, such as animals being overlooked and adopters facing mismatches in their search. By providing a platform where both parties can connect meaningfully, we hope to improve the overall experience for everyone involved.

Our app enables rescue centers to efficiently manage their animals by creating detailed profiles that highlight each petâ€™s temperament, needs, and care requirements. This helps ensure that potential adopters can easily find pets that fit their lifestyles and preferences. We believe that by focusing on compatibility rather than just appearance, we can significantly increase the chances of successful adoptions and provide a better quality of life for pets and their new families.

This blog serves as a valuable platform for us to gather insights and feedback from the community through interviews and discussions. Engaging with pet lovers, rescue centers, and potential adopters will help us refine our app to meet the unique needs of the pet adoption process. Through this collaborative effort, we aim to make pet adoption easier, more efficient, and ultimately more rewarding for everyone involved.
